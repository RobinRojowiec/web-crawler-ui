Wikipedia Webcrawlerv1.0


Supported Languages: English

A web crawler is a simple bot that can download and parse websites. It finds links in the HTML und adds them to a link list which then again is crawled. By defining the crawling depth in the settings, the crawler will be limited in the number of elements per follow links. In this demo, the crawler is specifically designed to crawl wikipedia pages and category pages from the english wikipedia.
